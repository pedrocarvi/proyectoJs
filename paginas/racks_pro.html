Rack Zeus

With the exclusive Froning Edition RML-4100C Power Rack, the Rogue team set out to create the ultimate, tailor-made CrossFit rack—featuring all of the unique elements preferred by the four-time CrossFit Games champion himself, Rich Froning, Jr.

The latest model of the RML-4100C shares the same primary Westside-inspired design as our standard RML-490C Color Rack 3.0 (3x3” 11-gauge steel, 5/8" SAE grade hardware, stabilizing back nameplate), but ups the ante with taller 100” uprights (ft. the Froning R* logo), a bare steel single pull-up bar for the best grip on high-rep workouts, and a denim-black-matte powder coat finish unlike anything else in the industry.

The 365 LB unit ships with black zinc hardware and band pegs, a pair of M-Lite J-cups, and a complete 43” Monster Lite Strap Safety Set included standard with each order. All uprights feature Westside hole spacing (1” through bench and clean pull zone, 2” spacing above and below) and precision numbering, and the crossmembers have been updated in the 3.0 style, making them compatible with our plate-loaded or band-resistance Slinger units. Like all of our Monster Lite racks, the Froning Edition can also be equipped with a wide range of other add-ons and accessories for easy customization and expansion.

Especificaciones

  • Uprights: 100.375" tall, laser-cut numbering
  • Finish: Exclusive Denim Matte Black inspired by classic motorcycles

  • Footprint: 53" x 53"
  • Westside hole spacing: 1” through bench and clean pull area, 2” spacing above/below
  • (1) 43" Single Skinny Pull-Up Bar – Bare Steel with black powder coat ends

  • (4) Band Pegs (Black Zinc) for additional resistance workouts

  • Important: Unit must be bolted to floor unless stabilizer is added. Mounting hardware is NOT Included.
